# PPC (Pay-Per-Click) Strategy

PPC strategy on Amazon goes beyond individual campaign metrics to consider the holistic relationship between paid advertising and organic sales performance. Effective PPC strategy recognizes that advertising investment impacts organic ranking and overall business health, not just immediate [ACOS](/metrics/acos.md) or [ROAS](/metrics/roas.md).

# Details

## Amazon's Sales Volume Algorithm

Amazon boosts product listings based on sales volume, creating a compounding effect where successful advertising can generate sustained organic benefits. This mechanism creates two types of sales cycles:

### Positive Sales Cycle

Increased ad spend → Greater ad sales → Improved organic ranking → More organic sales → Better overall performance

In this cycle, advertising investment generates both immediate ad-attributed sales and long-term organic benefits through improved product ranking and visibility.

### Negative Sales Cycle

Decreased ad spend → Lower ad sales → Reduced organic rankings → Lower organic sales → Diminished overall performance

This cycle illustrates the risks of cutting advertising spend too aggressively, as it can harm both paid and organic performance channels.

## Strategic Implications

The interconnection between paid and organic sales means effective PPC strategy should:

- **Consider [TACOS](/metrics/tacos.md) alongside ACOS**: Track total advertising cost against total revenue, not just ad-attributed revenue
- **Accept short-term ACOS increases**: New product launches and keyword research may require higher initial ACOS to build organic presence
- **Avoid over-optimization**: Setting too low of an ACOS target can prematurely eliminate keywords that would eventually convert and improve organic ranking
- **Focus on long-term growth**: PPC advertising serves as both immediate sales driver and investment in organic ranking improvement

## Snowball Effect

Amazon's sales-based ranking algorithm creates a snowball effect where PPC ads:

1. Increase product visibility through ad placements
2. Generate ad-attributed sales from interested shoppers
3. Improve organic ranking based on total sales volume
4. Lead to more organic searches and sales
5. Further reinforce product visibility and ranking

This mechanism makes PPC advertising a powerful tool for both immediate customer acquisition and long-term organic growth.
