# Metrics

- [ACOS](/metrics/acos.md) — advertising cost of sale, measuring ad spend as percentage of ad revenue
- [TACOS](/metrics/tacos.md) — total advertising cost of sale, measuring ad spend against total revenue
- [ROAS](/metrics/roas.md) — return on ad spend, measuring revenue earned per dollar of ad spend
- [CTR](/metrics/ctr.md) — click-through rate, measuring percentage of impressions that result in clicks
- [CPC](/metrics/cpc.md) — cost-per-click, measuring average cost paid per ad click
- [DPVR](/metrics/dpvr.md) — detail page view rate, Amazon-proprietary metric measuring product detail page views after ad exposure
- [ROI](/metrics/roi.md) — return on investment, measuring net profit earned per dollar spent
- [CPM](/metrics/cpm.md) — cost-per-thousand impressions, measuring cost to deliver 1,000 ad impressions
- [Impressions](/metrics/impressions.md) — number of times ads are rendered on pages or devices
- [Reach](/metrics/reach.md) — total number of unique users who see advertisements
- [Conversions](/metrics/conversions.md) — customer actions completed after engaging with ads
- [Brand Lift](/metrics/brand-lift.md) — measurement of advertising impact on shopper perception and behaviors
- [New-to-Brand Metrics](/metrics/new-to-brand.md) — measurements of first-time brand purchasers on Amazon
