# Sponsored Brands

Sponsored Brands is an Amazon advertising format that promotes brand messaging and multiple products through prominent placements in shopping results. These ads help increase brand awareness and drive consideration by reaching shoppers who are looking for what to buy.

# Details

## Keyword Strategy

For Sponsored Brands advertising campaigns, Amazon recommends including at least 25 keywords to maximize campaign reach and effectiveness. Keywords should include:

- **Phrases**: Multi-word search terms relevant to the brand
- **Broad keywords**: General terms that capture wider search intent
- **Product names**: Specific product terminology
- **Brand names**: Brand-related search terms

This comprehensive keyword approach helps Sponsored Brands campaigns capture relevant shopper searches and improve [ACOS](/metrics/acos.md) performance through better targeting.

## Campaign Performance

Sponsored Brands campaigns are measured using standard Amazon Ads metrics including:

- [ACOS](/metrics/acos.md): For Sponsored Brands, ACOS represents the ratio of ad spend to overall brand sales
- [CTR](/metrics/ctr.md): Click-through rate measuring ad engagement
- [Conversions](/metrics/conversions.md): Customer actions after ad engagement

The focus on overall brand sales (rather than just promoted product sales) makes Sponsored Brands particularly valuable for holistic brand building and awareness campaigns.
