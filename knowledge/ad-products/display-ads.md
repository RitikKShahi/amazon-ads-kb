# Display Ads

Display Ads are flexible ad formats that help brands connect with relevant audiences using Amazon's first-party insights and multi-format creatives. Display ads engage customers on Amazon properties and premium placements across the open internet, supporting both image and video formats.

## Note

Display ads were previously known as **Sponsored Display** before being rebranded as part of Amazon's unified display advertising offering.

# Details

## Overview

Display ads use audience-based behavioral targeting rather than keyword-based targeting to identify relevant users. They help advertisers discover, reach, and engage audiences across their shopping journeys with minimal campaign inputs and flexible cost structures.

Display ads leverage shopping and streaming insights on Amazon to reach customers when they are most ready to interact with brands, products, or services, using dynamically optimized creatives and premium placements to capture attention and create memorable moments.

## Platform Transition and Unification

Amazon has introduced a **single ad platform** that connects display campaigns with customers across:

- Amazon properties: Twitch, Fire TV, Echo Show
- Premium placements across the open internet
- Thousands of third-party apps and websites

The new **centralized hub** unifies access to all Amazon Ads products, bringing together sponsored ads and Amazon DSP workflows into a single workspace with simplified operations while preserving all existing advertising features.

### Campaign Access

- **Existing Campaigns**: Sponsored Display campaigns continue to run uninterrupted and can be accessed through Campaign Manager in the Ads Console
- **New Campaigns**: Navigate to 'Campaigns', click 'Create Campaign', then select 'Display' in the unified platform

## Availability and Eligibility

Display ads are available only to:
- **Vendors**
- **Professional sellers** enrolled in Amazon Brand Registry

However, display ads can be used by businesses that don't sell on Amazon, as demonstrated by case studies including pro sports teams.

## Ad Formats

Display ads support **both image and video formats**:

- **Image Ads**: Static or animated image creatives
- **Video Ads**: Video content for enhanced engagement
- **Dynamic Creative**: Automatically optimized creative elements

### Creative Optimization

- **Multi-product Optimization**: When multiple products are included, display ads automatically select creative elements with the highest conversion probability
- **Automatic Refresh**: Ad creatives automatically refresh when product detail page information changes
- **Amazon-generated Creative**: Can use Amazon-generated creative or custom creative assets

## Pricing Models

### Sponsored Ads (Self-Service)

Display ads through sponsored ads support:
- **Cost-Per-Click (CPC)**: Pay when customers click your ads
- **Cost-Per-Thousand Viewable Impressions (vCPM)**: Pay for 1,000 viewable impressions
- **No minimum spend requirements**
- **No management fees**
- **Default bids**: $1 for CPC campaigns, $5 for vCPM campaigns

### Amazon DSP (Managed Service)

Display ads executed through Amazon DSP:
- **Pricing varies** depending on format and placement
- **Typically requires minimum spend of $50,000** (may vary per country)
- **Managed service** with additional support

### Native Display Ads

Native display ads support multiple pricing models:
- **Share of Voice (SOV)**: Guaranteed impression commitments
- **Rotational Media**: Rotational placement among advertisers
- **Auction Pricing**: CPM, CPC, and cost per action models

## Budget Management

### Budget Requirements

- **No minimum spend requirements** for self-service display ads
- **Budget updates allowed** at any time
- **Daily budget calculation**: Daily spend may exceed the daily budget on any given day, but the monthly average will not exceed the set daily budget

### Budget Guidelines

Amazon recommends:
- **Daily budget of $40-60** for small to medium-sized businesses
- **Sufficient budget to stay live throughout the day** to avoid missing potential impressions
- **Campaigns without an end date** to avoid cutting results short

## Targeting Strategy

Display ads use **audience-based behavioral targeting** rather than keyword-based targeting.

### Contextual Targeting

Allows advertisers to target specific products or product categories within the Amazon store.

**Contextual Targeting Placements**:
- Product detail pages
- Customer review sections
- Shopping results pages

### Audience Targeting

Enables reaching shoppers based on their interests both on Amazon and third-party destinations.

**Amazon Audiences Categories**:
- **Interests**: Hobbies and interests shoppers engage with
- **Life Events**: Major life milestones and events
- **Lifestyle**: Behavioral patterns and preferences
- **In-Market**: Active shopping intent signals

### Remarketing Options

**Views Remarketing**: Re-engages shoppers who viewed products or similar products but did not purchase

**Purchases Remarketing**: Re-engages customers who bought products or complementary products to drive repeat purchases

### Targeting Combinations

- **Combining contextual targeting with display ads audiences can improve campaign results**
- **Amazon recommends including at least 10 products in display ad campaigns for optimal performance**
- **Advertisers promoting 10 or more products saw a 13% increase in sales over 30 days** compared to those who didn't

## Ad Placements

Display ads can appear on:

### Amazon-Owned Properties
- **Amazon homepage**
- **Product detail pages**
- **Amazon store** shopping results
- **Twitch** (live streaming platform)
- **Fire TV** (connected television)
- **Fire tablet** devices
- **Echo Show** devices
- **Prime Video** streaming service

### Third-Party Destinations
- **Thousands of third-party apps and websites**
- **Premium placements across the open internet**

### Placement Optimization

- **Automatic placement selection** based on targeting tactics
- **Optimized to help drive desired outcomes**
- **Device-specific experiences** across desktop, mobile, and tablet

## Campaign Creation and Management

### Creating New Campaigns

In the unified platform:
1. Navigate to **'Campaigns'**
2. Click **'Create Campaign'**
3. Select **'Display'** campaign type

### Bid Optimization Strategies

Display ads offer **three bid optimization strategies**:

1. **Optimize for Viewable Impressions**: Focus on ad visibility
2. **Optimize for Page Visits**: Drive traffic to product pages
3. **Optimize for Conversions**: Drive purchases (recommended for first campaigns)

### Performance Tradeoffs

- **Conversions optimization** typically results in fewer clicks compared to page visits optimization
- **Viewable impressions optimization** tends to generate higher view counts but lower click-through rates compared to other strategies
- **Conversions optimization is recommended** for first display ad campaigns

## Campaign Duration and Timing

### Performance Assessment

- **Wait at least two weeks** after campaign launch to assess performance with sufficient data
- **It takes 7-14 days** for product sales to be attributed to display ad clicks or views

### Campaign Duration

- **Amazon recommends running campaigns without an end date** to avoid cutting results short
- **Campaigns can be paused or adjusted** at any time based on performance

## Product Requirements

### Availability

- **Display ads automatically stop serving** when advertised products become unavailable for purchase
- **Ads resume automatically** when products become available again

### Promotional Features

Display ads can display **promotional badges** including:
- **Deal type badges** (e.g., Deal of the Day)
- **Coupons** promotional badges
- **Subscribe & Save** discount badges

## Performance Metrics

### Key Display Ad Metrics

Campaign performance is measured using:
- **Impressions**: Number of times ads are displayed
- **Spend**: Total advertising cost
- **Clicks**: Number of ad clicks
- **CPC**: Cost per click
- **vCPM**: Cost per thousand viewable impressions
- **CTR**: Click-through rate
- **Sales**: Attributed revenue
- **ACOS**: Advertising cost of sale
- **ROAS**: Return on ad spend
- **New-to-Brand**: First-time purchaser metrics

### Case Studies

**Pro Sports Team**: Display ads campaign increased impressions by 7 times and click-through rate by 70% three weeks post optimization.

**Thule Urban Glide 2 Stroller**: Two-month display ads with video test resulted in a 299% increase in average monthly glance views compared to the previous two-month period.

**Bagail**: Discoverability for brand name and products doubled from 2021 to 2022 while using display ads.

## Customer Journey

### Ad Click Destinations

When customers click display ads, they may be taken to:
- **Product detail page on Amazon**
- **Custom landing page** on an external website

### Attribution Window

- **7-14 day attribution window** for product sales attribution
- Sales are attributed to display ad clicks or views
- Supports both view-through and click-through attribution
