# Device Ads

Device ads are high-impact, immersive, and natively integrated advertising experiences that run across Amazon's ecosystem of devices with screens.

## Note

Device ads leverage Amazon's hardware ecosystem to deliver advertising in environments where customers are engaged with entertainment, shopping, and information services.

# Details

## Overview

Device ads extend Amazon's advertising reach beyond traditional web and mobile placements into the living room and other physical spaces through Amazon's connected device ecosystem. These ads provide immersive experiences that integrate natively with the device interface and user experience.

## Device Ecosystem

Device ads run across Amazon devices with screens including:

- **Fire TV**: Amazon's connected television platform
- **Fire tablet**: Amazon's tablet computing devices  
- **Echo Show**: Amazon's smart display devices
- **Prime Video**: Amazon's streaming video service

## Ad Experience Characteristics

Device ads deliver:

- **High-impact experiences**: Large screen formats and immersive environments
- **Natively integrated integration**: Ads that blend seamlessly with the device interface
- **Engaged audiences**: Users actively using devices for entertainment and information
- **Premium placements**: Access to valuable screen real estate in Amazon's ecosystem

## Strategic Value

Device ads provide advertisers with:

- **Living room access**: Reach customers in their home entertainment environment
- **Multi-screen engagement**: Complement web and mobile campaigns with connected TV exposure
- **Premium inventory**: Access to high-quality, brand-safe environments
- **Cross-device synergy**: Coordinate messaging across Amazon's device ecosystem
