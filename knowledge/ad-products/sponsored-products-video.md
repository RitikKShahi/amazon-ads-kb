# Sponsored Products Video Ads

Sponsored Products Video Ads enable advertisers to showcase products through video content within Sponsored Products campaigns, providing a more engaging format than traditional static product images. This format combines the targeting precision of Sponsored Products with the storytelling power of video creative.

# Details

## Video Format Specifications

### File Requirements

Sponsored Products video ads must meet specific technical requirements:

- **Duration**: Minimum 7 seconds required
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 layout (landscape orientation)
- **File formats**: MP4 or MOV formats supported
- **File size**: Maximum 500MB per video file
- **Resolution**: Standard HD quality recommended for best display

### Content Guidelines

Video content should:

- **Focus on the product**: Clearly showcase the advertised product
- **Maintain brand consistency**: Align with brand guidelines and messaging
- **Optimize for mobile**: Ensure content displays well on mobile devices
- **Include clear calls-to-action**: Guide viewers toward purchase decisions

## Video Asset Management

### Video Limits per ASIN

Advertisers can upload multiple videos for each product:

- **1-5 videos per ASIN**: Each product listing can have up to 5 video creatives
- **Creative testing**: Multiple videos enable A/B testing of different approaches
- **Performance optimization**: Identify which creative styles resonate best
- **Sequential messaging**: Test different storytelling approaches

### ATO Moderation Process

All video submissions undergo Amazon's ATO (Automated) moderation process:

- **Content review**: Automated systems check for policy compliance
- **Approval workflow**: Videos are reviewed before going live
- **Rejection handling**: Non-compliant videos are rejected with explanation
- **Resubmission process**: Advertisers can modify and resubmit rejected videos

## Performance Metrics

### Engagement Metrics

Video ads provide unique performance insights:

- **5-second views**: Metric tracking viewers who watch at least 5 seconds
- **View-through rate**: Percentage of impressions that generate meaningful views
- **Engagement quality**: Measures how thoroughly viewers consume content
- **Completion rate**: Percentage of viewers who watch the entire video

### Click-Through Rate Impact

Video ads demonstrate improved engagement compared to static formats:

- **9% CTR uplift**: Sponsored Products campaigns with video see average 9% higher CTR
- **Benchmark period**: Based on Amazon internal data from August-October 2025
- **US market data**: Performance improvements observed in US marketplace
- **Comparison baseline**: Video campaigns compared to image-only campaigns

## Bidding and Pricing

### Bid Boosting Capability

Video ads support bid adjustment features:

- **Bid boost range**: 0-900% bid boosting available
- **Default setting**: 0% means no bid adjustment applied
- **Maximum boost**: 900% allows up to 9x bid increase for video placements
- **Strategic use**: Higher bids can increase video ad visibility and placement

### CPC Pricing Model

Video ads operate under cost-per-click pricing:

- **Pay-per-engagement**: Advertisers pay when viewers engage with video ads
- **No impression costs**: No charges for video displays without user interaction
- **Bid-based placement**: Higher bids improve ad placement and visibility
- **Performance-based**: Costs aligned with actual customer engagement

## Performance Optimization

### Creative Best Practices

Effective video creative should:

- **Lead with product**: Show the product within the first few seconds
- **Demonstrate value**: Highlight key features and benefits visually
- **Maintain pacing**: Keep content dynamic and attention-grabbing
- **Include branding**: Ensure brand identity is clear and memorable

### Strategic Considerations

Video ad optimization involves:

- **Multiple creative variations**: Test different video styles and messaging
- **Performance monitoring**: Track view rates and engagement metrics
- **Audience targeting**: Combine video with precise keyword targeting
- **Budget allocation**: Ensure sufficient budget for video bid boosting
