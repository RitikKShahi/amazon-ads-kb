# Amazon Ads Platform Unification

Amazon has introduced a unified advertising platform that brings together sponsored ads and Amazon DSP capabilities into a single workspace, streamlining the advertising experience across Amazon's display advertising offerings.

# Details

## Overview

The platform unification represents a significant evolution in Amazon's advertising ecosystem, creating a centralized hub that unifies access to all Amazon Ads products while simplifying operations and preserving all existing powerful advertising features.

## Unified Campaign Access

### Single Ad Platform

Amazon has introduced **a single ad platform** that connects display campaigns with customers across:

- **Amazon Properties**: Twitch, Fire TV, Echo Show
- **Premium Placements**: Across the open internet
- **Third-Party Destinations**: Thousands of apps and websites

### Centralized Workspace

The new **centralized hub** unifies access to:
- **Sponsored Ads workflows** (Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, Display Ads)
- **Amazon DSP workflows** and capabilities
- **Cross-channel campaign management**
- **Unified reporting and measurement**

## Campaign Continuity

### Existing Campaigns

- **Uninterrupted Operation**: Existing Sponsored Display campaigns continue to run without interruption
- **Campaign Manager Access**: Users access existing campaigns through Campaign Manager in the Ads Console
- **No Migration Required**: Campaigns automatically transition to the unified platform

### New Campaign Creation

The unified platform introduces a streamlined campaign creation process:

1. Navigate to **'Campaigns'** in the Ads Console
2. Click **'Create Campaign'**
3. Select **'Display'** (or other ad format) in the unified interface

## Platform Benefits

### Simplified Operations

The unification delivers:
- **Streamlined workflow**: Single interface for all Amazon Ads products
- **Reduced complexity**: Eliminates need to switch between platforms
- **Consistent experience**: Unified navigation and campaign management
- **Preserved features**: All powerful advertising features remain available

### Enhanced Capabilities

The integrated buying experience combines:
- **Sponsored Ads strengths**: Self-service, flexible targeting, no minimum spend
- **Amazon DSP capabilities**: Programmatic buying, premium inventory, advanced measurement
- **Cross-platform insights**: Unified data and analytics

## Impact on Advertisers

### Operational Benefits

- **No learning curve**: Preserved functionality means existing processes continue
- **Flexible access**: Choose between self-service and managed-service options
- **Unified reporting**: Holistic view across campaign types
- **Streamlined management**: Single interface for all display advertising

### Strategic Advantages

- **Consolidated budget management**: Easier allocation across channels
- **Unified audience strategy**: Consistent targeting across platforms
- **Integrated measurement**: Cross-platform performance insights
- **Simplified workflow**: Reduced platform switching

## Migration and Transition

### Seamless Transition

The platform transition is designed to be:
- **Non-disruptive**: No interruption to existing campaigns
- **Automatic**: No manual migration required for most users
- **Preserved**: All campaign settings, bids, and targets maintained

### New Integrated Experience

The new **integrated ad buying experience** brings together:
- **Amazon Ads Console**: Self-service sponsored ads
- **Amazon DSP capabilities**: Programmatic display buying
- **Unified creative management**: Consistent creative across formats
- **Integrated measurement**: Cross-channel attribution and reporting
