# Platform Unification

Platform unification is Amazon's initiative to create a seamless, integrated ad buying experience that brings together Amazon Ads Console and Amazon DSP capabilities into a single, unified platform.

## Note

This unification allows advertisers to manage display campaigns that connect with customers across both Amazon properties and the open internet through a centralized workspace.

# Details

## Overview

Amazon is introducing a new seamless, integrated ad buying experience that brings together:

- **Amazon Ads Console**: The self-service sponsored ads platform
- **Amazon DSP**: The demand-side platform for programmatic advertising
- **Unified workspace**: Single interface for managing all Amazon advertising

## Platform Integration Benefits

The unified platform delivers:

- **Centralized campaign management**: Access all Amazon Ads products from one interface
- **Simplified operations**: Streamlined workflows for campaign creation and management
- **Preserved functionality**: All existing advertising features maintained
- **Enhanced capabilities**: Integration between sponsored ads and DSP workflows

## Campaign Capabilities

The unified platform enables:

- **Display campaigns**: Connect display ads with customers across Amazon properties and the open internet
- **Cross-property reach**: Seamless access to Amazon's first-party and third-party inventory
- **Integrated reporting**: Holistic view of campaign performance across all channels

## Transition Features

### Existing Campaigns

- **Continuity**: Existing campaigns continue to run uninterrupted
- **Access**: All campaign settings, bids, and targets are preserved
- **Management**: Same level of control as before, now through unified interface

### New Campaign Creation

- **Simplified process**: Navigate to 'Campaigns', click 'Create Campaign'
- **Unified options**: Select from all available Amazon Ads products in one place
- **Cross-platform capabilities**: Leverage both sponsored ads and DSP features seamlessly

## Strategic Implications

Platform unification represents Amazon's strategy to:

- **Eliminate fragmentation**: Single platform for all Amazon advertising needs
- **Improve user experience**: Simplified campaign management and reporting
- **Enhance capabilities**: Better integration between different Amazon advertising solutions
- **Scale operations**: More efficient campaign creation and optimization processes
