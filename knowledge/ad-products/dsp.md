# Amazon DSP

Amazon DSP (Demand-Side Platform) is a programmatic advertising platform that enables advertisers to purchase ads programmatically to reach both new and existing audiences across Amazon's properties and third-party destinations.

# Details

## Overview

Amazon DSP is available to brand advertisers, ad agencies, and tool providers, regardless of whether they sell products on Amazon. The platform leverages AI-powered optimization using first- and third-party insights to automate tasks and target relevant audiences on Amazon and across third-party apps, sites, and devices.

## Availability

Amazon DSP is available in **19 countries** across:

- **North America**: Canada (CA), Mexico (MX), United States (US)
- **South America**: Brazil (BR)
- **Europe**: Germany (DE), Spain (ES), France (FR), Italy (IT), Netherlands (NL), Poland (PL), Sweden (SE), Turkey (TR), United Kingdom (UK)
- **Middle East**: Saudi Arabia (KSA), United Arab Emirates (UAE)
- **Asia Pacific**: Australia (AU), India (IN), Japan (JP), Singapore (SG)

## Ad Formats

Amazon DSP offers **five main ad formats**:

- **Audio**: Audio advertising across Amazon's music and streaming platforms
- **Display**: Standard display advertising with IAB standard creative sizes
- **Online Video**: Video advertising across streaming and web properties
- **Streaming TV**: Television advertising on Prime Video and other connected TV platforms
- **Physical Store Advertising**: Digital signage in Amazon Fresh stores and other physical retail locations

## Inventory Sources

### Amazon's First-Party Supply

Amazon DSP provides access to premium supply across Amazon-exclusive properties including:

- **Prime Video**: Amazon Originals content
- **Twitch**: Livestreaming platform
- **Live Sports**: Including Thursday Night Football
- **Amazon.com**: Main retail platform
- **Amazon Fresh Kiosks**: In-store digital signage
- **Connected Devices**: Fire TV, Kindle, Alexa-enabled devices

### Third-Party Supply

Amazon DSP also provides access to third-party supply through:
- Direct integrations with publishers
- Leading supply-side platforms (SSPs)
- Premium placements across thousands of third-party apps and websites

## Pricing Models

Amazon DSP offers **two main pricing models**:

### Self-Service
- Customers have full control of campaigns
- No minimum spend requirements specified
- Direct access to platform features

### Managed Service
- Typically requires a minimum spend of **$50,000 USD**
- Minimum spend may vary per country
- Includes managed services and support

## Programmatic Buying Options

Amazon DSP supports **four programmatic media buying options**:

1. **Real-Time Bidding (RTB)**: Open auction with dynamic pricing
2. **Private Marketplaces (PMP)**: Curated inventory with publisher controls
3. **Private Auction**: Invitation-only auctions for premium inventory
4. **Programmatic Guaranteed**: Guaranteed impressions at fixed pricing

## Audience Targeting

Amazon DSP offers **three categories of audience targeting**:

### Amazon Audiences
Based on Amazon's first-party signals including:
- Shopping behavior
- Browsing activity
- Streaming habits
- Purchase history

### Advertiser Audiences
Driven by custom inputs including:
- Product relevance targeting
- Remarketing lists
- Custom audience segments

### Third-Party Audiences
Sourced from broader internet activities including:
- Demographic targeting
- Interest-based segments
- Behavioral data from external sources

## Campaign Management and Planning

### Media Planning Suite

Amazon DSP provides an integrated media planning suite with:

- **Cross-Channel Planner**: Allocate media across Amazon DSP supply sources
- **Channel Planner**: Simulate reach and budget in real-time
- **Optimization Tools**: AI-powered recommendations for budget allocation

### Measurement Solutions

Platform includes measurement capabilities such as:

- **Omnichannel Metrics (OCM)**: Holistic campaign performance measurement
- **Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM)**: Understanding campaign impact across channels
- **Viewability Metrics**: Standard viewability measurement
- **Reach and Frequency**: Audience measurement and frequency capping

### Reporting Metrics

Campaign reporting includes both industry-standard metrics and Amazon-proprietary metrics:

**Standard Metrics**:
- Impressions, clicks, conversions
- [CPM](/metrics/cpm.md), [CPC](/metrics/cpc.md), [CTR](/metrics/ctr.md)
- [ACOS](/metrics/acos.md), [ROAS](/metrics/roas.md)

**Amazon-Proprietary Metrics**:
- **DPVR** ([Detail Page View Rate](/metrics/dpvr.md)): Measures product detail page engagement
- **ATL** (Add-to-List): Shopping cart or wishlist additions
- **NTB** (New-to-Brand): First-time brand purchaser metrics

## Creative Options

Amazon DSP supports various creative formats:

- **IAB Standard Creative Sizes**: Common display ad dimensions
- **Custom Ads**: Bespoke creative executions
- **E-commerce Creative Templates**: 
  - Can include provided images
  - Can pull product images from detail pages
  - Dynamic creative optimization

## Platform Integration

Amazon DSP provides interoperability with key Amazon systems:

- **Amazon Web Services (AWS)**: Cloud infrastructure integration
- **Amazon Marketing Cloud**: Data collaboration and analytics
- **Amazon Publisher Cloud**: Publisher-side measurement and optimization

These integrations help eliminate fragmentation in the planning, buying, and measurement journey.
