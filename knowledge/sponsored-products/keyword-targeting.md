# Sponsored Products Keyword Targeting

Sponsored Products keyword targeting allows advertisers to display ads to shoppers based on specific search terms they use on Amazon. This targeting method connects ads to relevant customer searches, helping products reach interested shoppers.

# Details

## Targeting Mechanism

Keyword targeting enables advertisers to specify which search queries should trigger their ads. When a shopper's search matches the targeted keywords, the ad becomes eligible for display in search results and product pages.

### Keyword Assignment

Keywords are assigned at the **ad group level** within Sponsored Products campaigns. Advertisers can:

- Add multiple keywords to a single ad group
- Set individual bids for each keyword
- Organize keywords by product relevance or performance
- Group keywords to optimize campaign structure

### Native Language Support

Sponsored Products keyword targeting supports keywords in their native language without requiring translation. This allows advertisers to:

- Target shoppers using keywords in the original language of the marketplace
- Maintain keyword accuracy and cultural relevance
- Reach customers using locally appropriate search terms
- Avoid potential translation errors that could impact targeting precision

### Marketplace Scope

Keyword targeting operates within the specific marketplace where the campaign is configured. Keywords targeted in one marketplace (e.g., Amazon.com) do not automatically apply to other marketplaces (e.g., Amazon.co.uk), requiring separate keyword setup for each target marketplace.

## Negative Keyword Targeting

Negative targeting allows advertisers to prevent ads from showing for certain search terms. This feature helps:

- Exclude irrelevant or low-performing search queries
- Reduce wasted ad spend on uninterested shoppers
- Improve campaign focus on high-intent customers
- Maintain targeting precision and campaign efficiency

## Keyword Constraints

Sponsored Products keywords are subject to specific constraints and limitations:

- **Character limits**: Keywords must adhere to maximum character restrictions
- **Quality requirements**: Keywords must be relevant to the advertised products
- **Policy compliance**: Keywords must comply with Amazon's advertising policies
- **Performance standards**: Poorly performing keywords may become ineligible

## Strategic Considerations

Effective keyword targeting requires:

- **Research-based selection**: Choose keywords based on search volume and relevance
- **Match type optimization**: Use different match types to balance reach and precision
- **Bid management**: Set appropriate bids for different keyword performance levels
- **Regular refinement**: Continuously update keyword lists based on performance data
