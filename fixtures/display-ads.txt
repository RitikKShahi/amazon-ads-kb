## Frequently asked

questions, answered

How much do display ads cost?

Display ads executed through sponsored ads supports [cost-per-click (CPC)](https://advertising.amazon.com/library/guides/cost-per-click?ref_=a20m_us_p_da_faq_rs_lbr_gd_cpc) as well as [cost-per-thousand viewable impression (vCPM)](https://advertising.amazon.com/library/guides/cost-per-mille?ref_=a20m_us_p_da_faq_rs_lbr_gd_cpm) pricing options with no minimum campaign spend requirements. This self-service option gives customers full control of their campaigns with no management fees. Pricing for displays ads executed through Amazon DSP varies depending on the format and placement. This managed-service option typically requires a minimum spend of $50,000 (minimum may vary per country). Native display ads support share of voice (SOV), rotational media, and auction, such as CPM, CPC, and cost per action.

Where do display ads appear?

Display ads can appear in many locations across the Amazon store plus thousands of third-party apps and websites. For example, placements can be found on the Amazon homepage and product detail pages, owned and operated sites like Twitch and IMDb, and third-party destinations. Placements are automatically selected based on your targeting tactic and optimized to help drive your desired outcome.

Where are customers taken to when they click my ads?

Customers may be taken to a product detail page on Amazon or a custom landing page on an external website.

What type of formats can display ads support?

Display advertising supports images and video formats.

What happened to Sponsored Display?

We're introducing a new seamless, integrated ad buying experience that brings together the Amazon Ads Console and Amazon DSP capabilities. With this new experience, you can create campaigns that connect your display ads with customers across Amazon properties including Twitch, Fire TV, and Echo Show, as well as premium placements across the open internet. This new direction combines what was previously separated between Amazon DSP display campaigns and [Sponsored Display](https://advertising.amazon.com/solutions/products/sponsored-display?ref_=a20m_us_p_da_faq_p_sd) campaigns, delivering the simplicity you want while preserving all the powerful advertising features you've come to rely on.
